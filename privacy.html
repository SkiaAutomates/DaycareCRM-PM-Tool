Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information.

Effective Date: February 21, 2026

1. Introduction

Day Care Operation CRM ("we", "us", "our"), powered by Skia Automated Solutions (automatedsolutionz.com), is committed to protecting your privacy and the privacy of the families you serve. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share your information when you use our Service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you register, we collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Password (encrypted, never stored in plain text)
  • Organization name

2.2 Data You Enter Into the Service

As part of day care operations, you may enter:

Data Type Examples
Parent Profiles Names, email, phone numbers
Child Profiles Names, dates of birth, enrollment status
Attendance Records Check-in/out times, dates
Operational Data Classroom assignments, waitlists, projects

2.3 Payment Information

We do not directly collect or store your credit card or payment information. All payment processing is handled by Paddle.com, our Merchant of Record. Please refer to Paddle's Privacy Policy for details on their data handling.

2.4 Automatically Collected Data

  • Browser type and version
  • IP address (for security purposes)
  • Usage patterns (pages visited, features used)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the Service — displaying your data, processing your operations.
  • To authenticate your identity — login, session management.
  • To send essential communications — account confirmations, security alerts, billing notifications.
  • To improve the Service — analyzing usage trends to enhance features.
  • To ensure security — detecting and preventing fraud or unauthorized access.

4. Data Storage & Security

  • Your data is stored securely on Supabase cloud infrastructure with encrypted connections (TLS/SSL).
  • Each organization's data is isolated through Row Level Security (RLS) — no other organization can access your information.
  • Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms and are never stored in plain text.
  • We perform regular security reviews and updates.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties, except:

  • Paddle.com — for payment processing only.
  • Supabase — as our database and authentication provider.
  • Legal requirements — if required by law, regulation, or legal process.

6. Data Retention

  • Your data is retained for as long as your account is active.
  • If you cancel your subscription, your data is securely archived for 30 days before permanent deletion.
  • You may request immediate data deletion by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data.
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Delete your data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Export your data in a portable format.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at skia@automatedsolutionz.com.

8. Children's Privacy

While our Service stores information about children enrolled in day care programs, this data is entered and managed by authorized adult users (day care operators and staff). We do not knowingly collect information directly from children under 13. All child data entered into the system is subject to the same security and privacy protections described in this policy.

9. Cookies

We use local storage (similar to cookies) to maintain your login session and preferences. We do not use third-party tracking cookies or advertising cookies.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We will notify you of significant changes via email or an in-app notification.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: skia@automatedsolutionz.com
